The Best Movies According to Jithin Abraham

My top 3 movies of all-time

interstellar

Interstellar is a stunning sci-fi film about space exploration, time, and love. With breathtaking visuals, a powerful score, and strong performances, it’s both thought-provoking and emotionally moving.

Grans turismo

Gran Turismo is an exciting sports drama based on a true story. It follows a gamer-turned-professional racer, blending thrilling race sequences with an inspiring underdog journey. Fast-paced, emotional, and visually stunning!

RRR

RRR is a high-octane Indian action drama packed with epic fights, emotional moments, and stunning visuals. Featuring powerful performances and catchy music, it’s an entertaining rollercoaster ride!
